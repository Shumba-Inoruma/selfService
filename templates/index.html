About
CatchApp is a mobile application, developed and owned by Africom for making calls to all networks within Zimbabwe. It uses VOIP protocol. CatchApp is supported by both Android and iOS operating systems. This application is a pay-as-you-go with no monthly fee charged. For more info please visit our Facebook page and join thousands of Zimbabweans enjoying CatchApp!
